Unitr is designed to work without requiring any personal information from you. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Local Storage

All your conversion history, favorites, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. We do not store this data on any remote server.

Unitr allows you to copy conversion results to your device's clipboard. This is a user-initiated action only. We do not access, read, or monitor your clipboard at any other time.

5.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
