Privacy Policy

PixelKit - All-in-One Image Tools Studio

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Abdulghani Mawlood operates the PixelKit mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

Key Point: All image processing in PixelKit happens locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers. We do not collect analytics, crash logs, or any usage statistics.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

PixelKit is designed to work without requiring any personal information from you. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

The only user input we process are the images you select for editing and any text you type into tools such as Watermark, Text on Image, or WiFi QR Code. This data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the App, we may collect the following types of information:

Local App Data: Your favorites, recent tools, theme preferences, JPEG quality settings, auto-delete settings, and in-app review status are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

2.3 Device Information & Advertising Data

We may collect non-personal information about your device, including:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Device Advertising ID (for ad personalization)
  • App version and usage statistics (ad impressions, clicks)
  • Approximate location (IP-based, for regional ad targeting)

3. Data We Do Not Collect

PixelKit explicitly does not collect or process the following:

  • Your photos or images: All image processing is done locally on your device. We never access, upload, or store your images.
  • App interactions / Analytics: We do not track which tools you use, how often you use them, or your in-app behavior.
  • Crash logs / Diagnostics: We do not collect crash reports or performance diagnostics.
  • Personal identifiers: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • Location data: We do not track your GPS location. AdMob may use approximate location at the country/region level.
  • Contacts or messages: We do not access your contact list, SMS, or call history.
  • Browsing history: We do not track your web browsing activity.
  • WiFi credentials: WiFi QR Code tool uses your input locally to generate a QR image. We do not store or transmit network names or passwords.
  • EXIF metadata from images: The Image Metadata tool displays EXIF data locally on your device. This data is not uploaded or stored by us.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

4.1 Google AdMob

PixelKit uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data to provide personalized or non-personalized ads:

Data Type Purpose Shared With

4.2 Ad Formats Used

  • Banner Ads: Small ads shown at the bottom of the Home, Files, Favorites, and Settings screens.
  • Interstitial Ads: Full-screen ads shown occasionally after completing a tool action.
  • No Rewarded Ads: PixelKit does not use rewarded video ads requiring you to watch ads to unlock features.

4.4 Google Play In-App Review

PixelKit uses Google's In-App Review API to politely ask satisfied users to rate the App on the Play Store after several successful tool uses. This is entirely optional and respects your choice if declined.

5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (all processing is local)
  • Save your preferences (theme, favorites, JPEG quality) locally on your device
  • Remember your recent tools and favorites for quick access
  • Display relevant advertisements to support the App's ongoing development
  • Request optional Play Store ratings via Google's In-App Review API

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Local Processing

All image editing, compression, conversion, and other processing occurs entirely on your device. Your images never leave your phone. We do not operate any backend servers for image processing.

6.2 Temporary Files

PixelKit creates temporary output files during processing (e.g., converted images, compressed images, PDFs, collages, circle crops, comparison exports). These are stored in your device's temporary directory and can be previewed, saved, shared, or deleted using the in-app File Manager. The operating system may also clear temporary files automatically over time.

6.3 Ad Data

Advertising-related data (Advertising ID, approximate location, device info) is transmitted securely via HTTPS to Google's AdMob servers. We do not retain or store this data ourselves.

6.4 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Permissions We Request

PixelKit requests the following Android permissions:

  • Internet: Required for loading advertisements.
  • Camera: Required to capture photos directly within the app (e.g., Background Remover, Circle Crop, Collage Maker). We do not collect or upload camera data.
  • Write External Storage (Android ≤ 10 only): Required to save processed images to your device gallery on older Android versions. Modern Android uses scoped storage and does not require this permission.

Important: We do not request read storage permissions. Image selection uses the Android Photo Picker, which does not require storage access permissions on Android 13+.

  • The right to erasure ("right to be forgotten") — You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing — You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing — You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
